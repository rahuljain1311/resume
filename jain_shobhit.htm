I am a third year Ph.D. Candidate in Data Sciences and Operations department at USC Marshall School of Business co-advised by Prof. Ramandeep Randhawa and Prof. Kimon Drakopoulos in our department. Prior to joining USC, I completed my B.Tech in Production and Industrial Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi in 2016.
